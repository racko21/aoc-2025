# Day 8: Playground

Parse 3D junction box coordinates. Connect pairs by shortest Euclidean distance using Union-Find. Find the 3 largest connected components and multiply their sizes.

## Part 1
Connect the 1000 closest pairs. Multiply the sizes of the 3 largest resulting circuits.

### Example (10 connections → product of 3 largest = 5*4*2 = 40)
After 10 connections: circuits of sizes 5, 4, 2, 2, 1×7
