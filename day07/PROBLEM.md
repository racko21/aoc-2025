# Day 7: Laboratories

Tachyon beam enters at 'S' in the top row and moves downward. When it hits '^' (splitter), the beam stops and two new beams continue from immediately left and right of the splitter. Beams at the same column merge into one.

## Part 1
Count the total number of times a beam is split (i.e., how many splitters are actually hit by a beam).

### Example Input
```
.......S.......
...............
.......^.......
...............
......^.^......
...............
.....^.^.^.....
...............
....^.^...^....
...............
...^.^...^.^...
...............
..^...^.....^..
...............
.^.^.^.^.^...^.
...............
```

### Expected Answer: 21
